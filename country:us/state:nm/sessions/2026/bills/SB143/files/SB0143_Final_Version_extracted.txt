Section 1:
2 RELATING TO AGRICULTURE; UPDATING FEES FOR CERTAIN NEW MEXICO
3 DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE REGULATORY DUTIES.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
6 SECTION 1. Section 25-6-9 NMSA 1978 (being Laws 1963,
7 Chapter 138, Section 9, as amended) is amended to read:
8 "25-6-9. EGG INSPECTION FEE.--The Egg Grading Act shall
9 be financed in part by the collection of a fee on all eggs
10 sold to the retailer or consumer. The fee on eggs being sold
11 to the retailer or consumer shall be one-half cent ($.005)
12 per dozen; provided that the board may index the amount of
13 the fee for inflation until July 1, 2031 using the consumer
14 price index for all urban consumers: all items published by
15 the United States department of labor. The fee shall be paid
16 by the egg dealer or producer who packages the eggs for sale
17 to the retailer or consumer. All money collected under the
18 provisions of the Egg Grading Act shall be deposited with
19 New Mexico state university for the purposes of administering
20 the provisions of that act and promoting the poultry industry
21 and its products and shall be expended upon the order of the
22 board in the same manner as other funds of New Mexico state
23 university."
