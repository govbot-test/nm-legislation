HB0310 HOUSE BILL 310 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Susan K. Herrera and Kristina Ortez and Joseph L. Sanchez and Tara L. Lujan and Cynthia Borrego AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CULTURAL AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT FOR A FEASIBILITY STUDY AND PLANNING AND IMPLEMENTATION FOR A LOWRIDER MUSEUM IN ESPANOLA. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One million dollars ($1,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the cultural affairs department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 for a feasibility study and planning and implementation for a lowrider museum in Espanola. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .233593.1
