AN ACT

MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CULTURAL AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT FOR A FEASIBILITY STUDY AND PLANNING AND IMPLEMENTATION FOR A LOWRIDER MUSEUM IN ESPANOLA.

 

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:

     SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One million dollars ($1,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the cultural affairs department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 for a feasibility study and planning and implementation for a lowrider museum in Espanola. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund.
