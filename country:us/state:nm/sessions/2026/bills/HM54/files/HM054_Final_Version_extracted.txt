1 A MEMORIAL
3 WORKING GROUP TO DEVELOP RECOMMENDATIONS FOR A REGULATORY AND
4 STATUTORY FRAMEWORK THAT WOULD PROMOTE RELIABLE AND
5 CONSISTENT ACCESS TO UTILITIES FOR TENANTS RESIDING IN MOBILE
6 HOME PARKS.
7
8 WHEREAS, mobile homes make up roughly one-sixth of all
9 houses in New Mexico; and
10 WHEREAS, a significant portion of mobile homes in the
11 state are located within mobile home parks; and
12 WHEREAS, mobile home park residents often own a mobile
13 home while renting the land the home occupies from the mobile
14 home park owner; and
15 WHEREAS, the owner of a mobile home park is responsible
16 for providing utilities, including water, electricity, gas
and sewer, to a tenant's mobile home; and
17
WHEREAS, many mobile home park tenants have experienced
18
frequent and lengthy interruptions in access to utilities;
19
and
20
WHEREAS, there are currently few remedies for mobile
21
home park tenants who have limited or nonexistent access to
22
utilities; and
23
WHEREAS, the various utilities that serve mobile home
24
parks and mobile home park tenants are often under vastly
25 HM 54
Page 1
1 different regulatory schemes, with varying levels of
2 oversight from varying regulatory entities; and
3 WHEREAS, improved oversight and regulation are needed to
4 promote fair access to utilities in mobile home parks;
5 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF
6 REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO that the public
7 regulation commission be requested to convene a working group
8 to develop recommendations for a regulatory and statutory
9 framework that would promote reliable and consistent access
10 to utilities for tenants residing in mobile home parks; and
11 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the working group be
12 requested to be composed of a diverse group of stakeholders
13 that includes:
14 A. representatives from the public regulation
15 commission;
16 B. the superintendent of regulation and licensing,
or the superintendent's designee;
17
C. the attorney general, or the attorney general's
18
designee;
19
D. a representative from an association that
20
represents mobile home park owners;
21
E. a representative from an association that
22
represents mobile home park tenants;
23
F. a representative from a municipal utility; and
24
G. a representative from a group that advocates
25 HM 54
Page 2
1 for low-income persons; and
2 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the working group be
3 requested to report its findings, conclusions and
4 recommendations to the appropriate interim legislative
5 committees that study housing and property issues by November
6 1, 2026; and
7 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that copies of this memorial be
8 transmitted to the superintendent of regulation and licensing,
9 the chair of the public regulation commission and the attorney
10 general. HM 54
