Section 1:
SB0061 SENATE BILL 61 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Pete Campos and Gabriel Ramos and Leo Jaramillo and Martin R. Zamora AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION FOR RURAL LIBRARIES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Five million dollars ($5,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the cultural affairs department for expenditure in fiscal years 2027 and 2028 to support the operation of rural libraries; provided that no more than two million five hundred thousand dollars ($2,500,000) shall be spent in any one fiscal year. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2028 shall revert to the general fund. .233060.1
