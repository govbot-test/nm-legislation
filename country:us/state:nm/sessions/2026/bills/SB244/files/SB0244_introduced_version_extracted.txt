SB0244 SENATE BILL 244 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Antonio Maestas and George K. Muñoz AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF NEW MEXICO TO SUPPORT STUDENT ATHLETES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Five million dollars ($5,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the board of regents of the university of New Mexico for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to support student athletes, including travel, nutrition and overall student athlete needs. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .233650.2
