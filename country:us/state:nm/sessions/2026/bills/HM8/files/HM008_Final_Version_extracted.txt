1 A MEMORIAL
3 KING, JR.
4
5 WHEREAS, during the week of January 12 through January
6 19, 2026, the people of New Mexico will commemorate the life
7 and legacy of Reverend Doctor Martin Luther King, Jr.
8 throughout the state as part of the "Dream Week"
9 commemorations organized by the Martin Luther King, Jr.
10 commission, which was created by act of the legislature in
11 1991; and
12 WHEREAS, the theme for the observance of the 2026 Martin
13 Luther King, Jr. holiday is "Mission Possible Two: Building
14 Community, Uniting a Nation the Nonviolent Way"; and
15 WHEREAS, "Dream Week 2026" will feature events,
16 celebrations and moments of service designed to uplift Dr.
King's ideals and bring New Mexico communities together
17
statewide; and
18
WHEREAS, Dr. King was awarded the 1964 Nobel peace prize
19
for his leadership in the nonviolent struggle for civil
20
rights and the passage of landmark civil rights legislation;
21
and
22
WHEREAS, through his life, Dr. King left a blueprint to
23
follow for the achievement of both personal excellence and
24
realization of "the beloved community", where there is peace,
25 HM 8
Page 1
1 love, respect and equality for all; and
2 WHEREAS, it is important to honor Dr. King's teaching
3 that "injustice anywhere is a threat to justice everywhere";
4 and
5 WHEREAS, the diverse communities of New Mexico reflect
6 the beautiful multicultural vision of Dr. King's dream; and
7 WHEREAS, New Mexico is committed to civil rights and
8 equality for all; and
9 WHEREAS, Dr. King's philosophy of social justice and
10 nonviolence remains essential for addressing contemporary
11 challenges and building unity in communities;
12 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF
13 REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO that the life and
14 legacy of Reverend Doctor Martin Luther King, Jr. be
15 commemorated and honored; and
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that copies of this memorial be
transmitted to the governor, the president pro tempore of the
17
senate, the speaker of the house of representatives, the chair
