HB0189 HOUSE BILL 189 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Marianna Anaya AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CHILDREN, YOUTH AND FAMILIES DEPARTMENT TO PROVIDE STATEWIDE SUPPORT AND FAMILY AFFIRMATION SERVICES TO LESBIAN, GAY, BISEXUAL, TRANSGENDER AND QUEER FAMILIES AND EDUCATION AND OUTREACH SERVICES TO LESBIAN, GAY, BISEXUAL, TRANSGENDER AND QUEER PERSONS AND FAMILIES TO CONSIDER FOSTER PARENTING AND ADOPTION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Two hundred thousand dollars ($200,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the children, youth and families department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to provide statewide support and family affirmation services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and queer families and education and outreach services to lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender and queer persons and families to consider foster parenting and adoption. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. - 2 -
