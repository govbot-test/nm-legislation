Section 1:
SB0201 SENATE BILL 201 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Pete Campos AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION FOR RURAL LIBRARIES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One million five hundred thousand dollars ($1,500,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the cultural affairs department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to support the operation of rural libraries. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .233062.1
