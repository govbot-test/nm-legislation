HB0261 HOUSE BILL 261 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Luis M. Terrazas and Joy Garratt and Brian G. Baca and Gabriel Ramos and Tanya Mirabal Moya AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE PUBLIC EDUCATION DEPARTMENT FOR ACTIVITY BUSES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Three million dollars ($3,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the public education department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 for grants to small rural school districts for the purchase of buses for student activities. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .233370.2
