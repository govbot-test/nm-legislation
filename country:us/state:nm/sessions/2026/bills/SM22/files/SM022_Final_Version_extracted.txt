1 A MEMORIAL
3 WORKING GROUP TO STUDY STATE AND FEDERAL FUNDS ADMINISTERED
4 BY THE CHILDREN, YOUTH AND FAMILIES DEPARTMENT TO SUPPORT
5 DOMESTIC VIOLENCE SERVICES IN NEW MEXICO.
6
7 WHEREAS, domestic violence, also referred to as intimate
8 partner violence, remains a significant public safety and
9 public health issue in New Mexico, and the children, youth
10 and families department administers and distributes state and
11 federal funds intended to support domestic violence services
12 across the state; and
13 WHEREAS, transparency and accountability in the use of
14 public funds requires a clear understanding of how such funds
15 are administered and distributed, including who receives the
16 funds and for what purposes; and
17 WHEREAS, domestic violence providers across New Mexico
18 continue to report unmet needs and insufficient resources to
19 meet survivors' services demands;
20 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE
21 STATE OF NEW MEXICO that the legislative finance committee be
22 requested to convene a working group, including
23 representation from the department of finance and
24 administration, the legislative finance committee, domestic
25 violence providers from around New Mexico and the New Mexico SM 22
Page 1
1 coalition against domestic violence, to study all state and
2 federal funds administered by the children, youth and
3 families department for the purpose of supporting domestic
4 violence services in New Mexico; and
5 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that, at a minimum, the study
6 include:
7 A. an accounting of the state and federal
8 domestic-violence-related funds administered by the children,
9 youth and families department;
10 B. an examination of how those funds are
11 administered and distributed, including administrative
12 structures, pass-through arrangements and time lines;
13 C. identification of entities that receive such
14 funds and the amounts each entity receives; and
15 D. a recommendation regarding what level of
16 funding is needed to fully fund domestic violence support
17 services statewide with a focus on funding support being
18 directed to domestic violence providers; and
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that copies of this memorial be
20 transmitted to the legislative finance committee, the
21 secretary of finance and administration and the secretary of
22 children, youth and families. SM 22
