HB0268 HOUSE BILL 268 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Linda Serrato AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE CULTURAL AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT TO CONDUCT A FEASIBILITY STUDY FOR THE CREATION OF A STATEWIDE LAND GRANT AND ACEQUIA MUSEUM. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the cultural affairs department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to conduct a study on the feasibility and cost of creating a museum of land grants and acequias in the state that contemplates the purchase, lease or contracted use of land or buildings significant to land grants and acequias, including the Mercedes Unidas center, or locating the museum in the city of Santa Fe or the city of Albuquerque. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. - 2 -
