A RESOLUTION

ENACTING A NEW SENATE RULE REGARDING CONSUMPTION OF ALCOHOL.

 

     WHEREAS, Senate Rule 24-1 provides that the rules of the senate may be amended by a two-thirds' vote of all the members of the senate or by a majority vote of the members of the senate upon the recommendation of the senate rules committee;

     NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO that the following new Senate Rule 7-21 be adopted to read:

"7-21           No senator shall consume alcohol before or during any floor session or meeting of a committee to which a member has been appointed.".
