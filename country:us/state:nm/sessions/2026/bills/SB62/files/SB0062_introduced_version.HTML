AN ACT

MAKING A TRANSFER FROM THE GENERAL FUND TO THE RURAL LIBRARIES ENDOWMENT FUND; MAKING AN APPROPRIATION.

 

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:

     SECTION 1. TRANSFER.--Twenty-nine million five hundred thousand dollars ($29,500,000) is transferred from the general fund to the rural libraries endowment fund.
