SB0007 SENATE BILL 7 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Cindy Nava and Martin Hickey and Heather Berghmans and Katy M. Duhigg and Shannon D. Pinto AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF NEW MEXICO FOR THE HEALTH SCIENCES CENTER TO INCREASE CLINICIAN FACULTY PAY. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Twenty million dollars ($20,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the board of regents of the university of New Mexico for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 for the health sciences center to increase clinician faculty pay. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .232939.1
