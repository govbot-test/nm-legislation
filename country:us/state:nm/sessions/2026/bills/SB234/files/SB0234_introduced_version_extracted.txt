SB0234 SENATE BILL 234 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Nicole Tobiassen and Linda M. Trujillo and Candy Spence Ezzell AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE PUBLIC EDUCATION DEPARTMENT TO PROVIDE TRANSPORTATION TO AND FROM SCHOOL FOR FOSTER CHILDREN. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One million two hundred thousand dollars ($1,200,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the public education department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 and subsequent fiscal years for the Albuquerque public school district to provide transportation to and from school for foster children. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of a fiscal year shall not revert to the general fund. .232569.1
