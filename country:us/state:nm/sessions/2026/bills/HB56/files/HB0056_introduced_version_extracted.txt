Section 1:
HB0056 HOUSE BILL 56 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Alan T. Martinez and Rebecca Dow and John Block and D. Wonda Johnson AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE VETERANS' SERVICES DEPARTMENT TO ASSIST VETERANS AND THEIR FAMILIES IN IDENTIFYING AND ACCESSING BEHAVIORAL HEALTH CARE SERVICES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--One million dollars ($1,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the veterans' services department for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to assist veterans and their families in identifying and accessing behavioral health care services. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .232753.1
