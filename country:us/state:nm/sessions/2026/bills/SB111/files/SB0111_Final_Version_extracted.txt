Section 1:
2 RELATING TO MOTOR VEHICLES; AMENDING THE DEFINITION OF
3 "PERSONAL INFORMATION" IN THE MOTOR VEHICLE CODE.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
6 SECTION 1. Section 66-1-4.14 NMSA 1978 (being Laws
7 1990, Chapter 120, Section 15, as amended) is amended to
8 read:
9 "66-1-4.14. DEFINITIONS.--As used in the Motor Vehicle
10 Code:
11 A. "park" or "parking" means the standing of a
12 vehicle, whether occupied or not, other than temporarily for
13 the purpose of and while actually engaged in loading and
14 unloading;
15 B. "parking lot" means a parking area provided for
16 the use of patrons of any office of state or local government
17 or of any public accommodation, retail or commercial
18 establishment;
19 C. "parts car" means a motor vehicle generally in
20 nonoperable condition that is owned by a collector to furnish
21 parts that are usually nonobtainable from normal sources,
22 thus enabling a collector to preserve, restore and maintain a
23 motor vehicle of historic or special interest;
24 D. "pedestrian" means any natural person on foot;
25 E. "person" means every natural person, firm, SB 111
Page 1
1 copartnership, association, corporation or other legal
2 entity;
3 F. "personal information" means information that
4 identifies an individual, including an individual's
5 photograph, social security number, driver identification
6 number, name, address other than zip code, telephone number,
7 medical or disability information, sex, gender identity,
8 immigration status or national origin, but "personal
9 information" does not include information on vehicles,
10 vehicle ownership, vehicular accidents, driving violations or
11 driver status;
12 G. "placard" or "parking placard" means a
13 card-like device that identifies the vehicle as being
14 currently in use to transport a person with severe mobility
15 impairment and issued pursuant to Section 66-3-16 NMSA 1978
16 to be displayed inside a motor vehicle so as to be readily
17 visible to an observer outside the vehicle;
H. "platoon" means a series of motor vehicles that
18

19
with wireless communications or other technology allowing for
20
coordinated movement;
21
I. "pneumatic tire" means every tire in which
22
compressed air is designed to support the load;
23
J. "pole trailer" means any vehicle without motive
24
power, designed to be drawn by another vehicle and attached
25 SB 111

1 to the towing vehicle by means of a reach or pole or by being
2 boomed or otherwise secured to the towing vehicle and
3 ordinarily used for transporting long or irregularly shaped
4 loads such as poles, structures, pipes and structural members
5 capable, generally, of sustaining themselves as beams between
6 the supporting connections;
7 K. "police or peace officer" means every officer
8 authorized to direct or regulate traffic or to make arrests
9 for violations of the Motor Vehicle Code;
10 L. "private road or driveway" means every way or
11 place in private ownership used for vehicular travel by the
12 owner and those having express or implied permission from the
13 owner, but not other persons; and
