Section 1:
HB0299 HOUSE BILL 299 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Luis M. Terrazas and Gabriel Ramos and Jenifer Jones and Crystal Brantley AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION FROM THE GENERAL FUND TO THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION FOR THE SECOND PHASE OF A ROAD INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECT ON CERTAIN PORTIONS OF UNITED STATES HIGHWAY 180. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Three hundred fifty million dollars ($350,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the department of transportation for expenditure in fiscal years 2026 through 2029 for acquisition of rights of way, planning, design and construction and to match federal and other state funds for the second phase of road infrastructure project 1101841 on United States highway 180 to expand the highway from two lanes to four lanes between the city of Bayard and the city of Deming. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2029 shall revert to the general fund.
