SB0210 SENATE BILL 210 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Pete Campos AN ACT MAKING AN APPROPRIATION TO THE BOARD OF REGENTS OF NEW MEXICO HIGHLANDS UNIVERSITY TO CONSTRUCT A SOCCER FIELD FOR THE WOMEN'S SOCCER TEAM AND FOR FOOTBALL FIELD, TRACK, NATATORIUM AND GENE TORRES GOLF COURSE IMPROVEMENTS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. APPROPRIATION.--Eighty million dollars ($80,000,000) is appropriated from the general fund to the board of regents of New Mexico highlands university for expenditure in fiscal year 2027 to construct a soccer field for the women's soccer team and for football field, track, natatorium and Gene Torres golf course improvements. Any unexpended balance remaining at the end of fiscal year 2027 shall revert to the general fund. .233542.1
