Section 1:
SB0154 SENATE BILL 154 57th legislature - STATE OF NEW MEXICO - second session, 2026 INTRODUCED BY Katy M. Duhigg and Christine Chandler and Anita Gonzales AN ACT RELATING TO INSURANCE; ENACTING A NEW SECTION OF THE NEW MEXICO INSURANCE CODE TO REQUIRE THAT COVERAGE FOR LOSS FROM A WILDFIRE INCLUDE COVERAGE FOR LOSS FROM RELATED FLOOD DAMAGE. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: SECTION 1. A new section of Chapter 59A, Article 18 NMSA 1978 is enacted to read: "[NEW MATERIAL] PROPERTY INSURANCE--LOSS FROM WILDFIRE--COVERAGE.--When a policy covers damage from a wildfire as a covered loss, the policy shall include damage from related flooding and flood damage, including damage from landslides, debris flows, mudflows, mudslides or other similar events, as covered losses for five years after the wildfire is controlled." SECTION 2. APPLICABILITY.--The provisions of this act apply to policies issued on or after July 1, 2026. - 2 -
