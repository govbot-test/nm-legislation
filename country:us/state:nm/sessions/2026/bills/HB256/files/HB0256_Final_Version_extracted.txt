Section 1:
2 RELATING TO SCHOOL SAFETY; REQUIRING AUTOMATED EXTERNAL
3 DEFIBRILLATORS TO BE AVAILABLE DURING ALL SCHOOL ATHLETIC
4 ACTIVITIES.
5
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
7 SECTION 1. Section 22-33-6 NMSA 1978 (being Laws 2025,
8 Chapter 129, Section 3) is amended to read:
9 "22-33-6. CARDIAC EMERGENCY RESPONSE PLAN--AUTOMATED
10 EXTERNAL DEFIBRILLATOR.--
11 A. Each school shall develop and implement a
12 cardiac emergency response plan that addresses the
13 appropriate use of school personnel who are to respond to
14 incidents involving a person experiencing a sudden cardiac
15 arrest or a similar life-threatening emergency while on
16 school grounds or during a school athletic activity. Each
17 cardiac emergency response plan shall:
18 (1) be developed and implemented using core
elements that are nationally recognized and evidence-based;
19
(2) identify specific school personnel who
20
will take action when a person experiences a sudden cardiac
21
arrest or a similar life-threatening emergency;
22
(3) establish protocols for school personnel
23
identified by the cardiac emergency response plan to follow
24
when a person experiences a sudden cardiac arrest or a
25 HB 256
Page 1
1 similar life-threatening emergency;
2 (4) determine appropriate locations for
3 automated external defibrillators to be installed or made
4 available;
5 (5) provide for routine maintenance of the
6 automated external defibrillator; and
7 (6) be updated when necessary to be
8 consistent with evidence-based guidelines.
9 B. Every school in the state shall install an
10 automated external defibrillator. Installed automated
11 external defibrillators shall be maintained and tested
12 according to the manufacturer's operational guidelines.
13 C. An automated external defibrillator shall be
14 clearly marked and easily accessible during each school
15 athletic activity.
16 D. The department shall adopt and promulgate rules
for the training of school employees on the use of automated
17
external defibrillators in a manner consistent with standards
18
adopted by the American heart association.
19
