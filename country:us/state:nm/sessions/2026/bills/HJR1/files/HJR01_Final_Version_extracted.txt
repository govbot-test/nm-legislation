1 A JOINT RESOLUTION
3 CONSTITUTION OF NEW MEXICO TO ESTABLISH NOMINATING COMMITTEES
4 TO PROVIDE LISTS OF NOMINEES TO THE GOVERNOR FOR APPOINTMENT
5 OF NONSTUDENT MEMBERS TO THE BOARDS OF REGENTS OF STATE
6 EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS; PROVIDING THAT STUDENT MEMBERS OF
7 BOARDS OF REGENTS SHALL BE APPOINTED FROM A LIST PROVIDED BY
8 AN ELECTED STUDENT GOVERNING BODY OF THE INSTITUTION;
9 PROVIDING THAT, EXCEPT FOR A STUDENT MEMBER, MEMBERS OF THE
10 BOARDS OF REGENTS SHALL NOT HAVE CHANGED THEIR POLITICAL
11 PARTY REGISTRATION IN THE TWELVE MONTHS PRECEDING
12 APPOINTMENT.
13
14 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
15 SECTION 1. It is proposed to amend Article 12, Section
16 13 of the constitution of New Mexico to read:
17 "A. The legislature shall provide for the control
18 and management of each of the institutions, except the
19 university of New Mexico, by a board of regents for each
20 institution, consisting of five members, four of whom shall
21 be qualified electors of the state of New Mexico, one of whom
22 shall be a member of the student body of the institution and
23 no more than three of whom at the time of their appointment
24 shall be members of the same political party; provided that,
25 except for the student body member, members shall not have HJR 1/a
Page 1
1 changed their political party registration in the twelve
2 months preceding appointment; and provided further that the
3 student body member provision in this subsection shall not
4 apply to the New Mexico school for the deaf, the New Mexico
5 military institute or the New Mexico school for the blind and
6 visually impaired, and for each of those three institutions
7 all five members of the board of regents shall be qualified
8 electors of the state of New Mexico.
9 B. The governor shall nominate from a list of
10 names provided by a nominating committee for each
11 institution, as provided by law, and by and with the consent
12 of the senate shall appoint the members of each board of
13 regents for each of the institutions. The terms of
14 nonstudent members shall be for staggered terms of six years,
15 and the terms of student members shall be two years.
16 C. The governor shall select, with the advice and
17 consent of the senate, a student member from a list provided
18 by an elected student governing body of the institution.
19 D. The legislature shall provide for the control
20 and management of the university of New Mexico by a board of
21 regents consisting of seven members, six of whom shall be
22 qualified electors of the state of New Mexico, one of whom
23 shall be a member of the student body of the university of
24 New Mexico and no more than four of whom at the time of their
25 appointment shall be members of the same political party; HJR 1/a
Page 2
1 provided that, except for the student body member, members
2 shall not have changed their political party registration in
3 the twelve months preceding appointment. The governor shall
4 nominate from a list of names provided by a nominating
5 committee of the university, as provided by law, and by and
6 with the consent of the senate shall appoint the members of
7 the board of regents. The governor shall select, with the
8 advice and consent of the senate, a student member from a
9 list provided by an elected student governing body of the
10 university.
11 E. Members of the board shall not be removed
12 except for incompetence, neglect of duty or malfeasance in
13 office. Provided, however, no removal shall be made without
14 notice of hearing and an opportunity to be heard having first
15 been given such member. The supreme court of the state of
16 New Mexico is hereby given exclusive original jurisdiction
17 over proceedings to remove members of the board under such
18 rules as it may promulgate, and its decision in connection
19 with such matters shall be final.
20 F. The legislature shall provide for a nominating
21 committee for each state educational institution enumerated
22 in Article 12, Section 11 of this constitution to provide the
23 governor a list of nominees for appointment of nonstudent
24 members to the boards of regents of those institutions. The
25 legislature shall provide for the appointment, terms, powers HJR 1/a
Page 3
1 and duties of a nominating committee; provided that no more
2 than fifty percent of the members of a nominating committee
3 shall be members of the same political party; and provided
4 further that members shall not have changed their political
5 party registration in the twelve months preceding
6 appointment."
7 SECTION 2. The amendment proposed by this resolution
8 shall be submitted to the people for their approval or
9 rejection at the next general election or at any special
10 election prior to that date that may be called for that
11 purpose. HJR 1/a
