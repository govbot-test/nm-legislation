Section 1:
2 RELATING TO FINANCE; AUTHORIZING THE ISSUANCE OF BONDS
3 SECURED BY THE STATE GROSS RECEIPTS TAX DISTRIBUTION AND THE
4 GAMING TAX DISTRIBUTION FOR THE STATE FAIRGROUNDS DISTRICT
5 PURSUANT TO THE STATE FAIRGROUNDS DISTRICT ACT.
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO:
8 SECTION 1. Section 6-35-1 NMSA 1978 (being Laws 2025,
9 Chapter 83, Section 1) is amended to read:
10 "6-35-1. SHORT TITLE.--Chapter 6, Article 35 NMSA 1978
11 may be cited as the "State Fairgrounds District Act"."
12 SECTION 2. A new section of the State Fairgrounds
13 District Act is enacted to read:
14 "AUTHORIZATION OF ISSUANCE OF BONDS.--The legislature
15 authorizes and approves the district to issue bonds, pursuant
16 to the provisions of Paragraph (5) of Subsection C of Section
17 6-35-7 NMSA 1978, in an amount not to exceed ninety-two
18 million dollars ($92,000,000) in net proceeds of the five
19 hundred million dollars ($500,000,000) in net proceeds
20 authorized to be issued by the district by Subsection A of
21 Section 6-35-7 NMSA 1978, secured by all or a portion of the
22 gross receipts tax distribution and the gaming tax
23 distribution made pursuant to the provisions of Section
24 7-1-6.73 NMSA 1978, pledged to pay the principal of and
25 interest on the bonds, with such issuance of bonds by the SB 48
